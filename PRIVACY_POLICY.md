# Privacy Policy — Asma ul Husna: 99 Names of Allah

**Effective date:** 18 May 2026  
**Last updated:** 18 May 2026

---

## Who we are

Asma ul Husna: 99 Names of Allah ("the app") is an educational mobile application designed to help children learn the 99 Names of Allah through games, flashcards, and daily reminders. The app is developed and maintained as an independent project.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at:  
**Email:** [asmaulhusna.99.af@gmail.com]

---

## What data we collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All information created while using the app — including your learning progress, star count, streak history, earned badges, and notification preferences — is stored **only on your device** using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

---

## Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, the app stores your preferred reminder time **locally on your device**. We do not have access to this information. You can turn off notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app.

---

## Children's privacy

This app is designed for use by children. In line with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide:

- We do not collect any personal information from children or adults.
- We do not serve advertisements.
- We do not include any social features, in-app purchases, or links to external websites.
- We do not share any data with third parties.

Parents and guardians can allow their children to use this app with confidence that no personal information is collected or shared.

---

## Third-party services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

The app is distributed via **Google Play** and the **Apple App Store**. Those platforms may collect certain usage and device data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Please refer to Google's and Apple's privacy policies for details on data they may collect as part of operating their platforms.

---

## Data security

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored externally, there is no data at risk of a breach on our end. All locally stored progress data is protected by your device's built-in security.

---

## Your rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. If you wish to clear all app data, you can do so at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data through your device settings.

---

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
